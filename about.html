Our Story

Steel is not just material—it’s our promise. Based in Hosur, we deliver custom SS & MS fabrication built to last.

At YAS Fabrication, we specialize in stainless steel (SS) and mild steel (MS) fabrication with 25+ years of expertise. From railings, gates, balconies, and glass works to customized trolleys & pallets, our craftsmanship reflects precision and durability.

We believe in quality, trust, and long-term relationships. That’s why our customers continue to stay with us— because every project is built to last, with designs that blend strength and style.

Our work across Hosur—from Chennai Silks, SGR Groups, and ARRS Silks—stands as proof of our reliability and commitment to excellence.
